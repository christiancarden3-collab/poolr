How to Play
01
Create your pool

Choose a tournament, set your buy-in amount, define prize distribution, and configure payment settings. Takes under two minutes.

02
Invite your group

Share your private invite link via WhatsApp, SMS, or email. Players sign up and pay their buy-in instantly through Stripe.

Predict scores for each matchday before the deadline. Also submit your special picks — champion, top scorer, and best goalkeeper — before the tournament starts. All picks lock automatically.

Points are calculated automatically as results come in. Winners receive their payout via Stripe when the tournament ends.
